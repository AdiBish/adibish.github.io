About

I am a PhD student in the Centre for Neuroscience at Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore advised by Dr. Sachin Deshmukh and Dr. Sridharan Devarajan . My research focuses on studying how sensory information is integrated into the hippocampus spatial map. I use a combination of techniques including tetrode recording and computational modeling to understand contextual olfactory information processing in the brain.
